About Us

Our Mission

"Our mission is to bring healthcare of International standards within the reach of every individual. We are committed to the achievement and maintenance of excellence in education, research and healthcare for the benefit of humanity".
Cardio Diabetic and Chest Clinic was formed by Dr. Pankaj Kshatriya with an objective of “Preventing Heart and Diabetic diseases ”. Dr. Pankaj Kshatriya is India’s leading Cardiologist. Cardio Diabetic and Chest Clinic is pioneer in the field of Preventive Cardiology in Jabalpur and has done path breaking work in the field of Non-Surgical Treatments to treat Heart Ailments.
The mission of Cardio Diabetic and Chest Clinic is preventing Heart attacks reversing heart disease, through continuous education, early detection and provide alternative non invasive holistic treatment affordable by all and there by reducing the incidence of Coronary Artery Disease and Eradication of Premature deaths and disability.
The premier multi-specialty tertiary care hospital of Jabalpur has been acknowledged as center of medical excellence, The Cardio Diabetic and Chest Clinic is located in the heart of Jabalpur. The kind of confidence, trust it has developed on strong foundation of the state-of-the-art facilities, best medical expertise, and charitable endeavors; that today it serves patients from all walks of life. the Cardio Diabetic and Chest Clinic has focused its operation on providing quality care with a human touch; which truly reflects the essence of its motto “More than Healthcare, Human Care”.
