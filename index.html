Welcome

We are an independent cardiology clinic based in Surrey, specializing in the early diagnosis, investigation and treatment of patients with all forms of heart , nasal and diabetic diseases . With our advanced clinical facilities and friendly, professional staff, we are committed to providing you with excellence of service. Bringing a together a complete team of specialist nurses and consultants, dieticians and psychologists we provide high quality holistic care for the best treatment of cardiovascular disease. Our patient centered approach enables us to provide you with individualized healthcare specific to your needs, integrating medical, dietary, exercise and lifestyle approaches into a complete care package under one roof.

Our "one-stop" cardiology clinic makes it easier than ever to combine your consultation appointment with any necessary diagnostic tests into one patient visit. With a wide range of consultants and nurses working together, you are able to benefit from shared knowledge and expert opinions.
