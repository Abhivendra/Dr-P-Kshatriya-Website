Cardio Diabetic and Chest Clinic is a center of excellence where we see and assess people with suspected heart conditions. We have a dedicated team of Consultants, Nurses and Technicians that deal with heart conditions - and are therefore well placed to give advice about heart conditions and lifestyle measures that can be taken to prevent heart disease.

We do however appreciate that may people may be apprehensive about taking time off work to come and see us to find out about healthy living or to investigate minor symptoms.

Our service has therefore been created to take our teams into your workplace - if necessary after work in the evenings.

Our team comprises a Cardiologist and Specialist Nurses that will go out to groups of employees (15-200) in order to provide lectures, screening tests (Blood pressure and heart Rhythm screening) and advice.
